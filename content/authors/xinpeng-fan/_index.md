Xinpeng joined Dr. Lin's lab as a postdoctoral fellow in April 2025, supported by the China Scholarship Council (CSC). Before that, he worked at Hainan University (2022-present) and currently serves as a youth committee member of the Chinese Society of Rheology. He obtained his PhD in polymer materials from Zhejiang University in 2022. Xinpeng's research interests mainly focus on complex fluid rheology and the structure-property relationship of soft matters.
