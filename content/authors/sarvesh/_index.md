Sarvesh joined the Lin Group as a PhD student in August 2025. He holds an MTech in Polymers and Biosystems Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad and a BTech in Biotechnology from Vellore Institute of Technology. Driven by an interest in cellular microenvironments, his current research lies at the intersection of microfluidics, cell-environment interactions, and hydrogel platforms. Outside the lab, he is an avid traveller who loves exploring new places and trying diverse cuisines.
